KEVIN DURANT




    Kevin Wayne Durant, also known simply by his initials KD, is an American professional basketball player for the Brooklyn Nets of the National Basketball Association.
    

    Born: 29 September 1988 (age 32 years), Washington, D.C., United States
    Height: 2.08 m
    Shoe size: 18
    Weight: 109 kg
    NBA draft: 2007 (Round: 1 / Pick: 2)
    Current teams: Brooklyn Nets (#7 / Power forward, Small forward), United States of America, United States
