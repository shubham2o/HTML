RUSSEL WESTBROOK




    Russell Westbrook III is an American professional basketball player for the Los Angeles Lakers of the National Basketball Association. 
    He is a nine-time NBA All-Star and earned the NBA Most Valuable Player Award for the 2016–17 season.

    Height: 1.91 m
    Wingspan: 2.03 m
    Current team: Los Angeles Lakers (#4 / Point guard)
    Spouse: Nina Earl (m. 2015)
    NBA draft: 2008, Seattle Supersonics (Round: 1 / Pick: 4)
    Children: Noah Russell Westbrook, Skye Westbrook, Jordyn Westbrook
