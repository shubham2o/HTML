KLAY THOMPSON




    Klay Alexander Thompson is an American professional basketball player for the Golden State Warriors of the National Basketball Association.
    He is credited as one of the greatest shooters in NBA history.
    

    Born: 8 February 1990 (age 31 years), Los Angeles, California, United States
    Height: 1.98 m
    NBA draft: 2011 / Round: 1 / Pick: 11th overall
    Current teams: Golden State Warriors (#11 / Shooting guard, Small forward), United States of America
    Parents: Mychal Thompson, Julie Thompson
    Siblings: Trayce Thompson, Mychel Thompson
